Prepare to be amazed as IMPULSE 5.0 returns! This two-day National Event, hosted by the IEEE Student Branch of FISAT, is poised to unite the engineering community like never before. Brace yourself for an exhilarating lineup of technical workshops, captivating speaker sessions, and interactive activities.

With a rich legacy of five successful years, IMPULSE has solidified its position as the premier event for engineering students nationwide. The Techno-Managerial conclave features a dynamic blend of technical workshop sessions, invited speakers' sessions, and ice-breaking activities, attracting science and engineering graduates from every corner of India.

This year's event promises an unforgettable journey filled with excitement and innovation. Join us as we push the boundaries of possibility and explore new frontiers in the field of engineering. Don't miss your chance to be part of IMPULSE 5.0 the ultimate platform for growth and advancement!
